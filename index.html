Politeknik Ungku Omar, Ipoh

Politeknik Ungku Omar (PUO), established in 1969 in Ipoh, Malaysia, is one of the country's oldest and most prestigious polytechnics. It offers diploma programs in engineering, business, IT, and applied sciences, focusing on practical and industry-relevant skills. PUO provides various services, including career counseling, industrial training, and entrepreneurship support. The polytechnic also offers short courses, certifications, and industry collaborations to enhance employability. With modern facilities and strong industry ties, PUO remains a leading institution for technical and vocational education in Malaysia.

Politeknik Ungku Omar (PUO) offers a wide range of diploma programs in various fields to equip students with technical and practical skills for the workforce. Below are some of the fields offered:

  • Engineering & Technology
  • Information Technology & Computer Science
  • Business & Management
